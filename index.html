Hey, I'm Joe. I'm a Lead Game Designer based in San Francisco.

In my 10+ years of professional experience I've been lucky to work on some pretty cool projects across a bunch of different genres and design sub-disciplines. Hundreds of millions of players from around the world have played the games I've worked on.

Nowadays I'm leading a team of systems and combat designers at nWay. We make fighting games. As a design lead I advocate for game loops, monetization systems, and UX patterns that allow all types of players to have fun.

I'm currently available to take on design consultations. Just send me a message via email or LinkedIn.
